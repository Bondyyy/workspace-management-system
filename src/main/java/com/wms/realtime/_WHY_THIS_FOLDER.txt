TAI SAO realtime/ KHONG CO entity/ repository/ ?
=================================================
Vi WebSocket KHONG luu du lieu vao database.

realtime/ chi la "ong truyen tin" (message broker):
  Khi Space doi trang thai -> SpaceService goi realtime/ -> Phat tin cho tat ca client

Khong co gi de luu -> Khong can entity (khong co bang Oracle)
Khong co gi de doc/ghi -> Khong can repository

Day la pattern "Event Broadcasting":
  Nguon su kien (SpaceService) -> Kenh phat song (realtime/) -> Nguoi nhan (FE browser)
  Cung cach TV phat song: dai truyen hinh khong luu xem ai dang xem
